== Description ==

Imports products to your group in vk.com from your online store on Woocommerce.

Instructions for configuring the plugin:
[https://icopydoc.ru/nastrojka-plagina-import-products-to-vk/](https://icopydoc.ru/nastrojka-plagina-import-products-to-vk/?utm_source=wp-repository&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=import-products-to-vk&utm_content=readme&utm_term=instruction)
